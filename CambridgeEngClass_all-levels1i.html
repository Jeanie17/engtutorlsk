📘 Pre-Starters Grammar Foundations

Nouns (singular/plural: -s) Verb 'to be' (am/is/are) Basic adjectives (big/small) Simple questions (What? Where?) Numbers 1-20 & colours Prepositions: in, on, under Imperatives & common verbs

Key rules: Use 'a' / 'an' before singular nouns. Add -s for plurals (cat→cats). 'I am / you are / he is'. Introduce 'have got' positive and question forms.

📙 Starters Essential Grammar

Present simple (like/likes, play/plays) Can / can't for ability Possessive adjectives (my, your, his) Question words (who, which, how many) Prepositions: next to, between Plurals: -ies, -ves Present continuous (actions now)

Present simple for routines; present continuous for now; 'there is/there are'; adverbs of frequency (always, sometimes).

📒 Movers: Building Confidence

Past simple (regular & common irregular) Comparatives (bigger, more interesting) Going to for future plans Adverbs of manner (quickly, happily) Must / mustn't for obligation Object pronouns (me, him, them) Wh- questions in past

Past simple: -ed endings, irregular verbs (go→went, see→saw). Sequence connectors (then, after that). Comparatives and basic superlatives.

📕 Flyers: Towards Independence

Present perfect (experience, results) First conditional (if... will) Passive voice (basic) Reported speech (say/tell) Relative clauses (who, which, where) Modals: could, should, might Connectors: although, however

Introduce since/for, ever/never. Conditionals for real possibilities. Passive with present simple (English is spoken).
