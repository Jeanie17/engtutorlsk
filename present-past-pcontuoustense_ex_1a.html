Instructions: Complete all 50 exercises below. Pay close attention to verb forms, especially irregular verbs in the Past Simple tense. Use the "Check Answer" buttons to verify your responses.

📅
Present Simple

For habits, routines, facts

⏳
Present Continuous

For actions happening now

📚
Past Simple

For completed past actions

Key Formulas:

Present Simple: Subject + Base Verb (add -s/-es for he/she/it)
Present Continuous: Subject + am/is/are + verb-ing
Past Simple: Subject + verb-ed (or irregular past form)
