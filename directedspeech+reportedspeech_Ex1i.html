📢 Direct Speech · 直接引语
Rules:
• 直接引述说话人的原话，放在引号内。
• 常用逗号、问号、感叹号分隔，引述动词(said, asked, shouted等)可放在句首、句中或句末。
• 首字母大写，标点符号通常放在引号内。
Examples:
➤ She said, "I am happy."
➤ "What time is it?" he asked.
➤ "Let's go," Tom whispered, "before it rains."

🔄 Reported Speech · 间接引语
Key Changes:
• 去掉引号，用 that 连接 (可省略)。
• 人称、时态、时间/地点状语相应变化。
• 时态倒退: present → past; past → past perfect; will → would; can → could 等。
• 疑问句改为陈述语序，用 if/whether (一般疑问句) 或 wh-词。
• 祈使句用 tell/ask + to do。
Examples:
➤ Direct: "I love coffee," she said. → Reported: She said (that) she loved coffee.
➤ "Are you coming?" he asked. → He asked if I was coming.
➤ "Close the door," she told me. → She told me to close the door.
