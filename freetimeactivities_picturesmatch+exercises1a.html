2. Let's Make Questions! ❓

Question: Does he like dancing?
Answer: No, he doesn't.

3. Adverb Adventure! ✍️

Rewrite each sentence and put the adverb in brackets ( ) in the correct place.
Example:
I listen to music. (always) → I always listen to music.
I do my homework on Saturday. (never)
I am happy at the weekend. (always)
I meet my best friend at the cinema. (sometimes)
I go out on Saturday morning. (often)
The films are interesting. (usually)
